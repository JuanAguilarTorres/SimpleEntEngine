\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents the system that manages camera movement based on entity positions. 

The \doxylink{class_camera_movement_system}{Camera\+Movement\+System} updates the camera position to follow entities with a \doxylink{struct_camera_follow_component}{Camera\+Follow\+Component} and adjusts its position based on their \doxylink{struct_transform_component}{Transform\+Component}.
